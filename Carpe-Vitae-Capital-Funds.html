Australian Assets

Being a global company, we want to capitalize on investments worldwide.

British Assets

As a UK-based company, we strive to create wealth domestically by investing
in publicly listed companies on the London Stock Exchange.
We refer to this initiative as "Carpe Vitae British Assets" (CVBA).

At this moment, all of our British assets are cash or cash equivalents.
